All your calendars, one click

Connects to iCloud, Google, Outlook, Exchange -- any calendar synced to your Mac. Pick which ones count as "busy."

Smart time formatting

Outputs clean, human-readable times: "9-10:30am, 2-3pm." Rounds to clean boundaries. Skips declined meetings and free blocks.

Cross-timezone scheduling

Option+click to preview your availability and convert times to any timezone before copying. Your recipient sees their local time.

Keyboard shortcut

Ctrl+Shift+C copies your availability from anywhere -- no need to find the menu bar icon. Fully configurable.

Plain text or Markdown

Choose your format. Plain text for email. Markdown bullets for Slack and Notion. Switch in Settings or the preview popover.

Private by design

No accounts. No network access. No analytics. Your calendar data never leaves your Mac. App Sandbox with zero internet permissions.
